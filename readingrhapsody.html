Are you a beginning sight-reader who wants to become more confident? I'm currently working on a piano method series and I am ready to release my first sight-reading book!

Reading Rhapsody is a beginner sight-reading book aimed at teaching the fundamentals of reading music. This colorful 57 page book introduces new players to musical notation, time signatures, basic rhythms including eighth notes, and C-G of the Treble clef. It is suitable for beginning readers ages 8 to adult.

Reading Rhapsody includes workbook pages, such as drawing notes, writing in counts, and identifying note names and over 200 sight-reading exercises to ensure each concept is mastered and memorable.

These exercises are approached through a 2-step method:
1) Tap and say the note aloud with a steady beat.
2) Count the beats aloud and play the notes.

This 2-step method ensures that the reader learns the notes as well as the rhythms and can do them at the same time!

This is level 1 in what will become a series of levels designed to make the sight-reading process fun instead of daunting! My book is available in Solfege (Do, Re, Mi) OR letter names (A,B,C), whatever works for you!
